# ${ExitDo}

Exits a block of statements until started by [`${Do}`][1], [`${DoUntil}`][2] or [`${DoWhile}`][3].

## Syntax

    ${ExitDo}

## Example

    StrCpy $0 0

    ${Do}
        IntOp $0 $0 + 1
        ${If} $0 > 10
            ${ExitDo}
        ${EndIf}
    ${Loop}
